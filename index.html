A Journey of Resilience

2017

Visiting Scholar at ASU: Traveled to Arizona State University on a research grant as a geology doctoral student from the University of Buea, Cameroon. As civil conflict erupted in his home region, safety concerns prevented his return, prompting a complete life rebuild in the US.

2019 - 2020

Academic & Communal Anchor: Enrolled in ASU's Master of Advanced Study in GIS (MAS-GIS). Balanced working full-time with a 4.0 GPA, receiving the prestigious Balling Family GIS Fellowship Endowment, while dedicating his personal time to tutoring, reviewing resumes, and prepping classmates for successful careers.

2021

Telecom Career Launch: Began his career at Cox Communications as an HFC Design Engineer & GIS Analyst, while continuing to share his expertise with the next generation as a Part-time Faculty Associate at ASU.

Present (2026)

Leadership & Innovation: Continues to innovate as a Senior HFC Design Engineer at Cox, executing major spatial initiatives like the nationwide rock excavation model, while actively co-authoring mineralogical manuscripts.
